## PAT-001 | THE 3-DAY DISPOSAL

```PAT
ID:           PAT-001
NAME:         The 3-Day Disposal
CATEGORY:     INSTITUTIONAL

DESCRIPTION:
  When a government entity needs to officially close a file on
  something it actually intends to retain or absorb, it commissions
  a brief, documented "evaluation" that produces a negative finding.
  The evaluation must be:
  - Official enough to cite in future denials
  - Fast enough to precede outside scrutiny
  - Performed by someone with credentials but motivated conclusions
  
  The document this produces becomes the permanent public record.
  The actual disposition of the material happens separately,
  under different classification, by different personnel.
  The negative evaluation provides the plausible deniability layer.

EVIDENCE:
  - Trump evaluation: 2 days, 80 trunks, conclusion: "nothing of value"
  - Craigee's contradicting assessment never given official status
  - No second evaluation commissioned despite internal disagreement
  - The evaluation memo released publicly but authorization memo not

ANALOGUES:
  - Operation Paperclip: German scientists officially "denazified"
    in cursory evaluations before absorption into US programs
  - Roswell 1947: Weather balloon "evaluation" produced same day
  - CIA MKULTRA: Program officially "terminated" 1973, documents
    destroyed, some continuation documented later

STRENGTH:     5

PREDICTIVE:
  We will find that the authorization for Trump's evaluation
  (LED-002) was issued by someone with direct access to
  classified wartime programs — not a routine bureaucrat.
  The speed of the evaluation will be explained in internal
  documents as "wartime urgency" — which is true, but the
  urgency was to CLOSE the public record, not assess the work.

CONNECTED:    ENT-004, RED-003, LED-002
```

## PAT-002 | THE MOTIVATED EVALUATOR

```PAT
ID:           PAT-002
NAME:         The Motivated Evaluator Selection
CATEGORY:     INSTITUTIONAL / HUMAN NATURE

DESCRIPTION:
  When a specific outcome is needed from an evaluation,
  institutional actors select evaluators whose personal biases
  align with the desired conclusion. This is more reliable
  than bribery or coercion because:
  - The evaluator genuinely believes their conclusion
  - There is no corrupt transaction to expose
  - The evaluator's credentials remain intact
  - The outcome is psychologically self-consistent for the evaluator
  
  The selector doesn't need to say "find nothing."
  They simply need to choose the person most likely to find nothing
  based on existing animus, competitive instinct, or worldview.
  
  This is not conspiracy. This is bureaucratic intelligence.

EVIDENCE:
  - Trump's Van de Graaff humiliation by Tesla: documented
  - Trump's 2-day "nothing of value" conclusion: documented
  - No evaluator with pro-Tesla prior was offered the role
  - Craigee (positive assessment) was at different institution,
    under different authority, not part of the official record

ANALOGUES:
  - Einstein's illegitimate daughter: concealed because the people
    who knew chose not to ask, not because records were destroyed
  - The Warren Commission: critics note selection of commission
    members with specific political alignments
  - Congressional investigations routinely: committee chair
    selects sympathetic witnesses for key testimony slots

STRENGTH:     4

PREDICTIVE:
  The authorization document for Trump's selection (LED-002)
  will show either direct selection by someone who knew Tesla's
  work personally, or selection by committee where Trump's prior
  conflict was either known and ignored or actively preferred.

CONNECTED:    ENT-004, ENT-007, PAT-001, LED-002
```

## PAT-003 | THE CONVENIENT THEFT NARRATIVE

```PAT
ID:           PAT-003
NAME:         The Convenient Theft Narrative
CATEGORY:     INSTITUTIONAL / SUPPRESSION

DESCRIPTION:
  When an institution needs to discredit the primary witness
  to what materials existed before government seizure,
  a theft allegation serves multiple functions simultaneously:
  1. Discredits the heir/witness as unreliable
  2. Provides legal justification for extended government custody
  3. Creates a documented incident that explains any discrepancy
     ("materials were stolen, some not recovered")
  4. Places the institution in the role of victim rather than actor
  
  The allegation does not need to be proven or even prosecuted.
  The allegation itself, filed in official records, does the work.

EVIDENCE:
  - Kosanović theft allegation filed immediately post-death
  - Charge never prosecuted (Kosanović remained US-recognized diplomat)
  - Heavily redacted resolution — clean recovery claimed but not shown
  - Kosanović's subsequent cooperation with Belgrade museum
    suggests no actual criminal behavior found
  - FBI used theft allegation to justify continued material custody

ANALOGUES:
  - Asset forfeiture: theft allegation allows property seizure
    without conviction
  - COINTELPRO: allegations used to discredit civil rights leaders
    without prosecution
  - Historical pattern of "spy" allegations against
    scientists with foreign-born names (Oppenheimer, etc.)

STRENGTH:     3

PREDICTIVE:
  RED-002 (the heavily redacted Kosanović theft resolution)
  contains either proof that not all materials were recovered
  or proof that Kosanović saw evidence of prior US extraction.
  One of those two things explains why the resolution is classified
  when a clean recovery would not be.

CONNECTED:    ENT-003, RED-002, LED-007
```

## PAT-004 | THE INVENTORY DISAPPEARANCE

```PAT
ID:           PAT-004
NAME:         The Inventory Disappearance
CATEGORY:     SUPPRESSION / INSTITUTIONAL

DESCRIPTION:
  In classified material extraction, the cleanest method is not
  destruction but administrative reclassification.
  Material is not "taken" — it is "retained under different authority."
  The original inventory reflects the full count.
  The transfer inventory reflects the post-extraction count.
  The gap between them is explained as administrative error,
  packing consolidation, or left without explanation entirely.
  
  The genius of this method: there is no theft. There is no crime.
  There is only a discrepancy in paperwork, and paperwork discrepancies
  in federal agencies during wartime are unremarkable.

EVIDENCE:
  - 80 trunks in. 60 trunks out. 8 years. No reconciliation released.
  - OAPC mandate was meticulous inventory — gap is deliberate
  - No official explanation for discrepancy in released files
  - Seifer documents discrepancy from primary research but
    no counter-explanation has been offered officially

ANALOGUES:
  - Operation Paperclip: German scientists moved under false identities,
    their documentation altered or omitted
  - Church Committee findings: CIA destroyed records of
    assassination programs under cover of routine document management
  - MKULTRA: financial records routed through front organizations
    specifically to create inventory gaps in official books

STRENGTH:     5

PREDICTIVE:
  The National Archives OAPC records (LED-005) will contain
  one of: a transfer order (naming destination), a destruction
  authorization (naming authorizer), or a reclassification notice.
  The document exists. Finding it names the destination
  of the 20 missing trunks.

CONNECTED:    RED-004, LED-005, LED-001
```

## PAT-005 | THE REPUTATIONAL PRE-EMPTIVE STRIKE

```PAT
ID:           PAT-005
NAME:         The Reputational Pre-Emptive Strike
CATEGORY:     SUPPRESSION / HUMAN NATURE

DESCRIPTION:
  The most durable form of suppression is not silencing a person —
  it is making the world pre-emptively discount everything they say.
  
  This is accomplished by:
  1. Withdrawing positive institutional framing (funding, endorsement)
  2. Allowing "eccentric" narrative to fill the vacuum naturally
  3. Never directly attacking credibility — that creates sympathy
  4. Simply declining to engage, which signals to press and peers
     that the person is not worth engaging
  
  Once labeled eccentric, the target self-suppresses:
  legitimate claims are dismissed as more eccentricity.
  No further intervention needed.
  
  Tesla is the textbook case.

EVIDENCE:
  - Press framing shifts from "genius" to "eccentric" post-1904
  - Shift tracks precisely with Morgan funding withdrawal
  - No coordinated press campaign necessary — vacuum fills naturally
  - Later Tesla claims (death ray 1934) dismissed by press that
    previously celebrated him, without examining technical merit
  - The "eccentric" label meant his final 15 years of work
    received essentially no independent technical evaluation
    until after his death

ANALOGUES:
  - Einstein's later unified field theory work: dismissed as
    obsessive eccentricity by physics establishment,
    only now being partially reconsidered
  - Wilhelm Reich: eccentric label preceded and enabled
    his actual persecution (FDA book burning, 1956)
  - Barry Marshall: "eccentric" for claiming ulcers were bacterial
    (1980s) — won Nobel Prize 2005

STRENGTH:     4

PREDICTIVE:
  The "eccentric" narrative has a specific origination point —
  a publication or editorial decision that first used the framing
  and from which others copied it. Finding that origination point
  finds the institution or individual who initiated the framing.
  This is researchable through newspaper archive chronology.

CONNECTED:    ENT-001, ENT-005, ENT-008 (Edison)
```
